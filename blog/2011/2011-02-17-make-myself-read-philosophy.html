Why must I make myself read philosophy?

2011-02-17

Papers with authors in philosophy departments require a great deal of mental overhead and are a leading cause of my reading fatigue. It’s not that the ideas are intellectually challenging; I just wish they’d get to the point and pick up the pieces later. Maybe it’s just the dynamic duo of psych and philosophy that’s really to be avoided.
